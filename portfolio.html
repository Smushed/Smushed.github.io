BookWorm
Helping users attain their goal of reading more by building a community around what they would like to read. This application is created to help users create bookclubs online and keep everyone on track.

Bookworm allows users to create clubs, add their friends and assign books for them to read together. All the while there is a post mechanic which is unique to each group. This allows users of the groups to communicate and discuss the book online.
Bark!
Connecting users with other dog lovers in their area. Bark was designed to be mobile first. This app focuses on owners of specific breeds of dogs. A user signs up, adds the dogs they own to their profile and connects with others in their area with the same breed of dog.

Bark is focused creating groups where users can share ideas about their breed and their specific issues with that breed of dog. Bark also has a feature which you can mark your dog as lost and others in your area will be able to see.
Plan Easy
It's a terrible feeling sitting at home when you want to be out, Plan Easy connects you with your friends to sync your freetime. A single page web application has users create an account, and mark their freetime over the next seven days.

Plan Easy is built entirely on the front end, with Firebase handling the database as well as the user authentication.
