Fantasy Football Eliminator
Currently in Development
A fantasy football game that is a mix of daily and season long NFL fantasy. The goal is for you and your group to compete on a week to week basis on a game that lasts the entire season long.

Every week each player sets a roster with the restriction that they cannot play anyone they have not already played previously.
Bark!
A mobile web application for building a community of dog lovers. Users add dogs to their profile to gain access to groups for other users to talk with a community formed around their dog breeds.

Bark is focused creating groups where users can share ideas and specific issues related to specific breeds of dogs. Bark also has a feature which you can mark your dog as lost and others in your area will be able to see.
Plan Easy
It's a terrible feeling sitting at home when you want to be out. Plan Easy connects you with your friends to sync your freetime with theirs. A single page web application has users create an account, and mark their freetime over the next seven days.

Plan Easy is built entirely on the front end with Firebase handling the database as well as the user authentication.
